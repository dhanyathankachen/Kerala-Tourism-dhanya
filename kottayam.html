Kottayam

City of letters (Aksharanagri).

Lets Explore Kottayam..

Kottayam is an ancient city of Kerala. It is located in Kottayam district, one of the districts of the God’s Own Land. The city has a big contribution to print media and literature and hence rightfully referred as ‘Akshara Nagari’ meaning ‘city of letters’.
